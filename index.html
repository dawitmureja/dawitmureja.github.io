Dawit Mureja Argaw

Welcome! I recently completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at KAIST (Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology), where I was co-advised by Prof. Joon Son Chung and Prof. In So Kweon. I also received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST. During my Ph.D., I gained valuable research experience through internships at NVIDIA, Adobe Research (twice), and KAUST. I am also a recipient of the Jang Young Sil Postdoctoral Fellowship.

My research focuses on multimodal video understanding and efficient generative modeling, particularly for long-form media content.
