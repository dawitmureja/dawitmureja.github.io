I am a Ph.D. student co-advised by Professor In So Kweon and Professor Joon Son Chung , at Electrical Engineering, KAIST.

My research interests lie in the general areas of computer vision and machine learning with a particular focus on:

  • Image and video enhancement
  • Motion estimation
  • Video synthesis and compression
  • High-level video understanding
  • Video editing and summarization
  • Video generation
